# Starbound

## Problem

Engineering students struggle to balance academics and placement preparation.

Students often don't know:

- How many classes they can safely miss
- Whether they are on track for their CGPA goals
- What subjects deserve the most attention
- How prepared they are for placements
- What they should focus on each day

Current solutions are fragmented across college portals, spreadsheets, calendars, and notes.

Starbound aims to provide a single intelligent platform that helps students make better academic and placement decisions.

## Target User

Primary Users:

- Engineering students in India
- Students preparing for placements
- Students managing CGPA and attendance requirements

Secondary Users:

- University students in other disciplines

## Core Promise

Starbound helps students answer:

"What should I focus on right now to reach my academic and placement goals?"

## Features

### Academic Planning

- Attendance Tracker
- Safe Skip Calculator
- Exam Calendar
- CGPA Planner

### Placement Planning

- Placement Readiness Dashboard
- Skill Tracking
- Roadmaps

### Scheduling

- Personalized Study Planner
- Weekly Focus Recommendations

## MVP Features

1. Attendance Tracker
2. Safe Skip Calculator
3. CGPA Planner
